Does the parrot recognize music?

Milan Jelic

Enough fun with these colorful storytellers! Parrots, in addition to being cheerful and beautiful, are known as great pets that brighten everyone's day. These birds are not difficult to raise, and even if you think they are, we are sure that they will give you a good argument why you are wrong! In addition to talking, there are indications that parrots recognize music. Is it true or false? Find out below.

Scientists confirm: Parrot recognizes music!

It's official: the parrot has a great sense of rhythm! After studying the cockatoo's cockatoo, which danced to famous pop songs from over 1,000 YouTube videos, scientists noticed for the first time that the parrot "plays" in accordance with the rhythm of the songs. If you think that this is a coincidence, you should also know that research has shown that other animals do not have such a good sense of rhythm. For example, with the exception of parrots, it happened that some elephants had a sense of music, until evidence was found that dogs and cats, even after a long exposure to music, did not feel the rhythm.

Music expert: Parrot has a refined musical taste

When it comes to their musical taste, parrots can be very picky - who would say! Parrot recognizes music, so he recognizes what he likes. Research has shown that some parrots prefer classical music, while some prefer to listen to pop music. In any case, it was determined that parrots do not like dance music at all.
